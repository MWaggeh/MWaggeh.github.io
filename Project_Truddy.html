TRUDDY

Need travel buddies? Truddy is an app that considers filters to connect people with similar travel interests and lets them get to know each other and plan the trip at the same time! An online travel get together.

MY ROLE

The whole design process, from ideating the app to prototyping and testing.

THE PROBLEM

We have all been in the situation where we've wanted to travel to a specific destination with some friends, relatives or our couple but it seemed impossible or hard to get them to go due to factors such as budget, time of the year or desirability of the place to visit, here is where Truddy came into a concept.

THE USER

The target in this case is quite specific, professional people aged 24-35. This persona is someone who is open to new experiences and getting out of his/her comfort zone.

THE OPPORTUNITY

A social platform where people interested in finding travel buddies can match people with similar interests. With Truddy, people can easily talk through the chat to create that engagement and keep the spark on. Considering how busy this user usually is, the app also helps with the trip organization by allowing to create an itinerary with the different activities all travel buddies are interested in fullfilling.

KEY FUNCTIONALITIES
Add your trip

Add a destination you wish to go and dates and filters for the type of user you would like to travel with so that only users with similar characteristics can join you.

Chat with your travel buddies

Before travelling it is important to get to know each other as affinity is key. The chat allows to do so, users can talk with potential travel buddies via message,call or even videocall and learn about each's personalities and see whether there is compability.

Plan your itinerary

Now, we all know that time is needed to organize a trip, things are made simpler with the itinerary planner, so everything is integrated. Users can chat and organize at the same time. Spots to see, new activities they didn't know about, they can all contribute and easily schedule the different activities they wish to carry out througout the trip.
